@.weld/conventions.md

This repo manages a personal Claude Code statusline configuration. It exists to track history and make deliberate, reviewed changes.

**Philosophy:** Changes here are intentional. Keep edits focused and minimal — one change at a time, clearly reasoned. No speculative additions.

**Scope:** Statusline config only. Do not add tooling, scripts, or unrelated files without explicit instruction.

**Voice:** Match the repo's tone — personal but professional. Clear over clever.
